Peace N Quiet

Tone therapy for tinnitus symptoms and doctor-guided treatment tracking

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Nature of the Application

Peace N Quiet is a health application designed to provide tone therapy for individuals experiencing tinnitus symptoms. The app also enables your doctor to monitor and track your treatment progress for better care.

Information We Collect

  • Therapy Data: Information about your therapy sessions, such as session times, duration, and progress.
  • Treatment Progress: Data tracked by your doctor regarding your response to therapy and overall treatment outcomes.
  • Account Information: Name, email, and other details needed to create and manage your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve tone therapy and treatment tracking features.
  • To enable your doctor to monitor your treatment and provide personalized care.
  • To communicate with you about your account or therapy.

HIPAA Compliance & Data Privacy

We are committed to protecting your health information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other applicable privacy laws. We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data and only share it with your healthcare provider as necessary for your treatment.

Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time.
  • You may request a copy of your therapy and treatment data.
  • If you have questions about your privacy, contact us at support@pnqhealth.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
